## Risk Scoring

### Probability Scale (1-5)

| Rating | Level | Probability | Description |
|--------|-------|------------|-------------|
| 5 | Very High | 80-100% | Will almost certainly occur |
| 4 | High | 60-79% | Likely to occur |
| 3 | Medium | 40-59% | May occur |
| 2 | Low | 20-39% | Unlikely to occur |
| 1 | Very Low | 0-19% | Rare, but possible |

---

### Impact Scale (1-5)

| Rating | Level | Financial Impact | Schedule Impact | Quality Impact |
|--------|-------|-----------------|-----------------|----------------|
| 5 | Catastrophic | >$1M loss | Program failure | Data breach, major outage |
| 4 | Major | $500K-$1M | >3 month delay | Major customer impact |
| 3 | Moderate | $100K-$500K | 1-3 month delay | Team morale hit |
| 2 | Minor | $10K-$100K | <1 month delay | Temporary disruption |
| 1 | Negligible | <$10K | No delay | Minimal disruption |

---

### Risk Score Calculation

```
Risk Score = Probability (1-5) √ó Impact (1-5)

Risk Severity:
- Critical:  16-25 (Immediate action required)
- High:      12-15 (Active mitigation, close monitoring)
- Medium:    6-11  (Monitor, contingency plans)
- Low:       1-5   (Accept or minimal mitigation)
```
