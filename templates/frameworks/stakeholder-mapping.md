## Escalation Path

### Escalation Tiers

**Tier 1: Program Manager**
- Handles: Day-to-day stakeholder concerns, routine decisions
- Escalates to Tier 2 if: Cannot resolve within 1 week, high-power stakeholder involved

**Tier 2: Executive Sponsor (CTO)**
- Handles: High-power stakeholder concerns, budget decisions, strategic issues
- Escalates to Tier 3 if: Board-level concern, cross-organizational conflict

**Tier 3: CEO / Board**
- Handles: Existential program risks, major organizational conflicts
